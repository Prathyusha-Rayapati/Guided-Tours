Guided Locations

New York City

The iconic skyline of NYC boasts the Statue of Liberty, Central Park, and Broadway, making it a cultural and financial hub.

Niagara Falls

One of the most famous waterfalls in the world, Niagara Falls offers boat tours and stunning views from both the US and Canada.

Grand Canyon, Arizona

Carved by the Colorado River, the Grand Canyon offers breathtaking vistas and unparalleled hiking opportunities.

Yellowstone Park, Wyoming

Home to geysers, hot springs, and diverse wildlife, Yellowstone is the oldest national park in the world.

San Francisco, California

Known for the Golden Gate Bridge and diverse neighborhoods, San Francisco blends natural beauty with urban charm.

Las Vegas, Nevada

The Entertainment Capital of the World, Las Vegas dazzles with its mega-casinos, shows, and vibrant nightlife.

Mount Rushmore, SD

This colossal sculpture features the faces of four U.S. presidents carved into the granite of the Black Hills.

Miami, Florida

Renowned for its Art Deco architecture, white sandy beaches, and vibrant nightlife along Ocean Drive.

New Orleans, Louisiana

Jazz music, Creole cuisine, and historic French Quarter architecture define the unique charm of New Orleans.

Chicago

The Windy City boasts world-class museums, architecture, deep-dish pizza, and stunning views from the Willis Tower Skydeck.

Boston

A city rich in American history with landmarks like the Freedom Trail, Boston Tea Party Ships, and Harvard University.

Seattle, Washington

Famous for the Space Needle, Pike Place Market, and its coffee culture, Seattle offers stunning views of Puget Sound.

Alaska

With rugged wilderness, glaciers, and abundant wildlife, Alaska is a paradise for nature lovers and adventurers.

Washington, D.C

The capital city of the United States features iconic landmarks like the White House, Capitol Hill, and Smithsonian museums.

Sedona, Arizona

Known for its red rock formations, spiritual retreats, and scenic hiking trails amidst the Coconino National Forest.
